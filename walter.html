Space Adventure

About the Game

Space Adventure is an action-packed shooter where you pilot a spaceship through dangerous asteroid fields and enemy fleets. Collect power-ups, upgrade your ship, and survive as long as you can in this endless space odyssey!

Controls

W Move Up
A Move Left
S Move Down
D Move Right
SPACE Shoot
P Pause Game
